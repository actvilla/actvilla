Branding Offer

  1. Home
  2. Branding

Your company branding in a new market at Intex South Asia would be an excellent opportunity for your organisation to establish its presence and increase your visibility in the growing South Asian Apparel & Textiles industry. You can choose from one of our top-line branding opportunities, giving you an advantage to put your company’s brand in front of key industry buyers from South Asia and other international markets showcasing your exclusive and innovative spring summer collection.

The unique branding opportunities, however, are limited in number, hence we will be awarding them solely on first come, first serve basis.

You can choose from the below listed Branding opportunities at Intex South Asia –

  1. Glam Galleria (Self-standing Branding)
  2. Fashion Forward (Wall Panel Branding)
  3. Lightbox at Registration Area, Trends Zone or Food Court.

Special Benefit – If you choose more than 1 branding option, we would be pleased to offer 5% package discount for your brand presence at Intex South Asia.
